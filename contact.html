About Me

Born and raised in central Illinois, but proud to call the Windy City home since 2013. Graduate of Illinois State University. Currently a GIS Analyst and map junkie with an extreme interest and excitement in web development. Enjoy listening to all types of music, especially Bluegrass. An early riser and coffee enthustiast, weekends are generally spent watching my favorite sports (soccer, hockey, football), going on runs, searching for a spot with local brews, or lacing up my skates as a fourth-line beer-league all-star. With a thirst for knowledge, a ton of motivation, and a love of new technology, I am very much looking forward to what the future holds within web development.
